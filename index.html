🤖 Research Projects

Computer Vision

VisionLearner

❓ Problem: Traditional ML models underperform on complex image data. Can deep learning architectures generalise more effectively?

A comparative analysis of CNN architectures — from simple CNNs to EfficientNet — on MNIST and CIFAR‑10.

📊 Result: EfficientNetB0 achieved 80.3% accuracy on CIFAR‑10, outperforming standard CNNs by 2.2%.

Natural Language Processing

TextSense

❓ Problem: How do transformer‑based models compare on sentiment analysis across different domains?

Evaluates BERT, RoBERTa, XLNet, ALBERT, DistilBERT, and ELECTRA on IMDb, Twitter, and Amazon datasets.

📊 Result: RoBERTa achieved 93.4% accuracy on IMDb — the highest among models tested.

Explainable AI

ModelExplainer

❓ Problem: Black‑box models are powerful but opaque. How can we make them interpretable without sacrificing performance?

A unified framework comparing SHAP, LIME, Integrated Gradients, DeepLIFT, LRP, and Grad‑CAM.

📊 Result: SHAP provides the most consistent global explanations (cosine similarity = 0.99).
