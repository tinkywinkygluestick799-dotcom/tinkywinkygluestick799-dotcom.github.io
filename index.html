About Me

I am a student researcher with an interest in quantum computing, mathematics, and theoretical physics. My work focuses on quantum error correction, stabilizer codes, and computational simulations.

Featured Research

Quantum Error Correction and Stabilizer Codes

This research compares the performance of Shor’s 9-qubit code and Steane’s 7-qubit code under depolarizing noise. The project combines mathematical analysis with Python-based simulations.

Quantum Circuit Simulation with Qiskit

This project explores quantum circuit optimization and simulation using IBM's Qiskit framework. It focuses on implementing basic quantum algorithms like Deutsch-Jozsa and Grover's search.

Quantum Error Mitigation for Near-Term Devices

This research develops and benchmarks error mitigation techniques like zero-noise extrapolation and probabilistic error cancellation for noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices. The goal is to enable meaningful quantum computations before full fault-tolerance is achieved.
