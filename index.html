About Me

I am a student researcher with an interest in quantum computing, mathematics, and theoretical physics. My work focuses on quantum error correction, stabilizer codes, error mitigation, and computational simulations. I aim to contribute to the development of practical quantum computing through both theoretical analysis and hands-on implementation.
