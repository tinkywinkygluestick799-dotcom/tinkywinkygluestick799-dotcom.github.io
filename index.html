👩‍🔬 About Me

I'm a 14‑year‑old researcher from Karachi, Pakistan, with a passion for quantum computing and artificial intelligence. I taught myself quantum mechanics, linear algebra, and Qiskit using free online resources — from YouTube lectures to IBM's documentation. My first quantum simulation ran on a laptop, not a supercomputer.

I've since built independent research projects in quantum error correction and NISQ device mitigation, and I'm now expanding into classical AI/ML. My goal is to bridge the gap between quantum physics and machine learning — building algorithms that will run on tomorrow's hardware.

Compares Shor's 9-qubit code and Steane's 7-qubit code under depolarizing noise.
