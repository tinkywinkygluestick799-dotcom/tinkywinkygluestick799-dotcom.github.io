🔬 Quantum Error Mitigation for Near-Term Devices

A Comprehensive Study of Noise Reduction Techniques in NISQ Quantum Computers

📋 Abstract

This research presents a comprehensive analysis of quantum error mitigation techniques for Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) devices. We implement and benchmark three state-of-the-art methods: Zero-Noise Extrapolation (ZNE), Probabilistic Error Cancellation (PEC), and Readout Error Mitigation. Our experiments on IBM's ibm_osaka quantum processor demonstrate that combined mitigation strategies reduce expectation value errors by up to 76% compared to unmitigated results.
